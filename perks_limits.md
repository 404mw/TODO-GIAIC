## Acheivements (Badges and Perks)
- daily login (streak based)
   - 3 days: +1 daily free ai credits
   - 7 days: +2 daily free ai credits
   - 15 days: +3 daily free ai credits
   - 30 days: +4 daily free ai credits
- completing tasks
   - 5 tasks: +15 max tasks
   - 10 tasks: +20 max tasks
   - 25 tasks: +25 max tasks
   - 50 tasks: +25 max tasks
- completing tasks in focus mode
   - 3 tasks: +2 subtasks per task
   - 10 tasks: +4 subtasks per task
- notes
   - Convert 5 notes to tasks: +5 notes
   - Convert 10 notes to tasks: +5 notes
   - Convert 15 notes to tasks: +5 notes
- Voice mode
   - spend 5 minutes in voice mode: no perk but the user is given a badge
   - spend 15 minutes in voice mode: no perk but the user is given a badge
   - spend 25 minutes in voice mode: no perk but the user is given a badge


## AI credit cost
